Hello, HMIA.

We start with four "Fireside Chats about Four Alignment Moments" (a few of them literally so). The goal is for students to "discover" an array of important alignment concepts and we finish by asking how these get tweaked when a new kind of agent with different natural limits enters the picture. Then we do "LYSK about Social Science" where we learn the names of these concepts and flesh out their content, especially the idea that they are always achievements and don't happen because we can build something into the agents. And then (not sure in what order): "LYSK about Alignment", "LYSK about AI."
